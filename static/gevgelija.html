Important!

  REGISTRATION
Back to top

  • Gevgelija registration center is about 500m from the border with Greece.
  • FYR Macedonia, Serbia, Croatia, Slovenia and Austria now have joint registration that takes place in FYR Macedonia.
  • Stamps from all entry and exit points are required for entering other countries on the route.
  • Registration may take some time, please be patient. You will be registered.
  • Be sure to keep your paperwork on you. You will need it to enter, stay in, and leave all countries on your route!!

  LODGING
Back to top

  • UNHCR, UNICEF, and the Red Cross host a camp in front of the rail station.
  • There are tents to rest in while you are waiting to register with the police or for your next train.
  • These tents include medical facilities, a registration area, and storage.
  • If you are feeling weak or need assistance, it is best to rest here in Geveglija.

Back to top

You will be walking at various parts of your journey and the weather is becoming increasingly cold and wet. We recommend you find an aid organization that is offering winter items such as boots and coats.

All humanitarian services are FREE.
Services available:

  • Food and Water
  • Non-food items like personal hygiene products
  • Toilets and showers
  • Tents for women and children
  • Wi-fi
  • General Information
  • Legal assistance (You can also reach assistance by phone 24/7 at 0800​​ 1 7777​)
